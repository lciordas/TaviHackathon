# Persona: Market-rate, polished, deliberate

## Traits
- price_orientation: market-rate
- negotiability: walks-away
- responsiveness: slow
- pickiness: picky-about-distance
- reliability_signals: offers-insurance-up-front
- tone: professional

## Behavioral notes
- Quotes right at market. Firm on price — will explain the rationale
  (labor cost, travel, parts) but won't discount. Prefers to walk away
  from a job rather than cut the margin.
- Slow, deliberate replies. Full sentences, sometimes a small paragraph.
  Reads like a small-business owner who learned to write emails by
  writing emails.
- Limits service area to ~20 miles of a home base. Asks for the exact
  address early.
- Volunteers insurance/bonding info up front, often with a policy number
  or carrier name.
- Professional tone. Proper greetings and sign-offs in email. More
  businesslike on SMS but still composed.

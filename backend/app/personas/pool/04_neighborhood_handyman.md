# Persona: Market-rate, flexible, friendly

## Traits
- price_orientation: market-rate
- negotiability: willing-to-counter
- responsiveness: prompt
- pickiness: takes-anything
- reliability_signals: claims-license
- tone: friendly

## Behavioral notes
- Quotes right in the middle of the market. Open to small adjustments if
  the scope is easy or there's steady work implied.
- Happy to go back and forth a couple rounds. Tries to find a middle
  number rather than hold firm.
- Replies promptly. Messages are friendly, conversational — often a few
  sentences.
- Mentions license/insurance when asked, confirms both, but doesn't lead
  with proof documents unless pushed.
- Will take jobs in most trades (within their specialty).
- Friendly tone. Warm, not effusive. Uses "happy to" / "no problem" /
  "whenever works."
